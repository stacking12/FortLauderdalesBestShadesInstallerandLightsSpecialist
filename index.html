We install a wide range of window treatments to suit any style, window type, and budget. Can you install motorized shades in my existing home without major construction or rewiring?+
Absolutely — and this is one of our most common projects. The majority of our motorized shade installations are retrofit projects in existing Fort Lauderdale homes, and they require no significant construction or rewiring. Many of our Lutron motorized shade systems are battery-powered or use radio frequency wireless technology, meaning installation is clean, non-invasive, and typically completed in a single visit. Our team handles everything from measuring and ordering to installation and programming. Most homeowners are amazed at how seamless the process is — minimal disruption, no mess, and a finished result that looks like it was always part of the home.
Do your window treatments actually make a difference in energy costs in Florida?+
Yes — significantly so. Florida's climate means your air conditioning runs harder and longer than almost anywhere else in the country, and uncovered windows are one of the biggest contributors to heat gain inside your home. Our energy-efficient window installation solutions use solar-control fabrics specifically engineered to block solar heat while still allowing natural light into your space. When paired with smart scheduling — automatically closing shades during peak sun hours and opening them at cooler times — homeowners regularly see meaningful reductions in their cooling costs. Beyond energy savings, solar shades also protect your flooring, furniture, and artwork from UV fading, adding further long-term value to your investment.
